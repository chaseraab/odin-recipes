White People Tacos

White People Tacos are a staple of caucasian families all over the United States. Introduced in the seventies from America's favorite resturant chain, Taco Bell, WPT are an excellent source of protein, carbs y un pocito spice (bueno).

I remember, growing up in the slums of the North Shore, my parents would make WPT as a celebration for my siblings and I. Such occasions including wining our high school track meets or demolishing the neighborhood children in Yu Gi Oh.

Ingredients

  • Hard shells (no soft shells)
  • Cheese
  • 2 lbs of ground beef
  • Lettucs
  • Onion
  • 1 tub of sour cream
  • 2 packets of Taco Bell taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Bring sauce pan to heat on medium
  2. Place beef in pan and begin to cook, breaking into smaller chunks
  3. While beef cooks in sauce pan, dice onions and cut onions
  4. Beef is done cooking when entirely browned. Pour excess grease into disposal receptical or directly into your gullet you disgusting animal.
  5. Follow directions on seasoning packet for ground beef seasoning and completion.
  6. Bake hard shell tacos for a bit in the oven, but not too long because they will burn
  7. Serve tacos by scooping completed beef into taco shell, topped with cheese, lettuce, onion and generous amounts of sour cream

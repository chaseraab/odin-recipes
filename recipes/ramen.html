Cup of noodles is the best meal for anyone who lacks the motivation to make a real meal. Cup of noodles, Japan's self entitled "Greatest Invention of the 20th Century" is gurenteed to satisfy any broke college student during a late night of exestential dread.

The meal, if you can call it that, typically comes in chicken or beef flavors but more are being thought up and marketed everyday!

Ingredients

  • Cup of Noodles

Steps

  1. Bring 1 cup of water to a boil
  2. Pour cup of water into cup of noodles
  3. Wait 3 minutes (or eat instantly for noodles al dente)
  4. Enjoy

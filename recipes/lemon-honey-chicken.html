Lemon Honey Chicken

Lemon honey chicken is the best god damn fucking chicken you will ever have in your pathetic peasant life. Its super easy to make to the point you will not eat anything besides this deliction dish for every single one of your meals.

I don't need to write two paragraphs to convince you of how amazing this dish is. Just make it and taste it for yourself. Then throw all other food in your kitchen away since you no longer need it.

Ingredients

  • Whole lemon
  • Enough (but not too much) honey
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Italian Seasoning

Steps

  1. Combine lemon, honey, oilive oil, salt, pepper, garlic poweder and italian seasoning in a bowl
  2. Stir Ingredients to combine
  3. Place chicken in bowl
  4. Toss chicken to coat in ingredients
  5. Let sit as marinade for 24 hours
  6. Once ready for cooking, preheat pan or grill to medium heat
  7. Place chicken on pan
  8. Let cook for 10 minutes before flipping chicken and cooking for an additional 6 minutes
  9. Cook until internal temperature has reached 165 degrees farenheight
